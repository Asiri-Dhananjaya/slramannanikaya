Vesak Celebration 2024

Join us for the annual Vesak celebration, a day of great significance for Buddhists worldwide. The event will include Dhamma talks by senior monks, guided meditation sessions, and opportunities for almsgiving.

Schedule

  • 9:00 AM: Opening Ceremony
  • 10:00 AM: Dhamma Talk
  • 11:30 AM: Almsgiving
  • 2:00 PM: Guided Meditation
